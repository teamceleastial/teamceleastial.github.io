Roona Sans is a modernist typeface with wide curves. 
Drawn and created by Måns Grebäck in 2018, this space aged type comes in ten different styles; 
Thin, Light, Medium, Bold and Black - and each weight as italic. 

The font has support for hundreds of languages, ligatures and high-quality vectors.
